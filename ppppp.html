Extension Manager Instructions

Follow these steps to access extension permissions:

  1. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions
  2. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right corner
  3. Find your extension in the list and click "Details"
  4. Scroll down to "Site access" permissions
  5. Select "On all sites" to allow full access
